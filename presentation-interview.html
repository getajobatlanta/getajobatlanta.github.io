How to Present Yourself

Explain how you will meet the employer’s needs

Engage in the conversation!

a. Ask doubts when you have them, about the history and context of the position, what a day in the new job looks like, etc.

b. Be genuine and asking questions back shows your interest in the position and the interview.

Do’s and Don’ts

a. DO dress professionally and be your most authentic, professional self.

b. DON’T beg for the job.

c. DO arrive a few minutes early.

d. DON’T wear overbearing scents.

e. DO clarify doubts you have on questions and know the names of your employers.
